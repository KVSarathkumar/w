Wife:Sakshi

Dhoni and Sakshi incidentally met each other at the Taj Bengal, during Team India's stay in November-December, 2007. At that time, India was playing against Pakistan at the Eden Gardens. Sakshi Rawat, who was an intern working at Taj Bengal was introduced to MS Dhoni, by his manager, Yudhajit Dutta.

Sakshi is truly MSD's lucky charm. She has been there for him always as a pillar of strength. After his marriage to Sakshi, Dhoni has captained two ICC World Cups for India, and gone on to winning the most cherished one in 2011.

Daughter:Ziva

Ziva Singh Dhoni (Born: 6 February 2015) is the daughter of Indian Cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni & Sakshi Singh Dhoni from Ranchi, Jharkhand, India.She is famous for being the Daughter of Cricketer MS Dhoni & Sakshi Dhoni. Ziva Singh Dhoni was born on 6 February 2015 and age is 5 years as of 2020.
